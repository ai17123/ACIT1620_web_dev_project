How to Play Webmon

Welcome to Webmon! Here’s how you can play the game and battle your opponent:

  1. Attack: Deal damage to the enemy. Use this option to weaken your opponent.
  2. Defend: Reduce the damage taken from the enemy's attack by using your defense.
  3. Heal: Restore some of your health. Be strategic, as your opponent might attack during this turn.
  4. Forfeit: Surrender the game, and declare the enemy as the winner.

HOW TO WIN: The game ends when one player’s health reaches zero.

TIP: Try to choose your moves wisely and defeat the enemy!
